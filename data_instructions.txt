# 如何获取粉丝和关注的 HTML 数据 / How to Gain Followers and Following Data from Instagram (Legally)

以下是合法获取 Instagram Follower 和 Following 数据的详细步骤：

---

## 中文版
1. **登录你的 Instagram 帐号**
   在浏览器中访问 https://www.instagram.com 并登录你的账号。

2. **进入设置页面**
   - 点击右上角头像图标，选择“设置与隐私”。
   - 找到“您的活动”，点击进入。

3. **下载信息**
   - 滚动到底部，找到“下载您的信息”选项。
   - 选择“部分信息”，并勾选“粉丝和关注”。
   - 选择“下载到设备”，时间范围选择“所有时间”。

4. **接收邮件**
   - 提交后，Instagram 会通过电子邮件发送数据下载链接。
   - 打开邮件，点击链接下载 ZIP 文件，并解压。

5. **找到数据文件**
   - 在解压后的文件夹中，进入 `connections/followers_and_following`。
   - 你会找到两个 HTML 文件：`followers.html` 和 `following.html`。

6. **将文件用于项目**
   - 将这两个文件复制到项目目录，准备运行脚本进行分析。

---

## English Version
1. **Log in to Your Instagram Account**
   Visit https://www.instagram.com in your browser and log in to your account.

2. **Navigate to Settings**
   - Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Settings and Privacy."
   - Find "Your Activity" in the menu and click it.

3. **Download Your Information**
   - Scroll to the bottom to find "Download your information."
   - Select "Some of your information," and check the box for "Followers and Following."
   - Choose "Download to device," and set the data range to "All time."

4. **Receive the Data**
   - After submitting the request, Instagram will send an email with a download link.
   - Open the email, click the link, download the ZIP file, and extract it.

5. **Locate the Data Files**
   - In the extracted folder, navigate to `connections/followers_and_following`.
   - You will find two HTML files: `followers.html` and `following.html`.

6. **Use the Files in the Project**
   - Copy these two files into the project directory to prepare for analysis with the scripts.

---

以上步骤完全合法并符合 Instagram 的隐私政策，请勿将数据用于未授权用途。
These steps are fully compliant with Instagram’s privacy policy. Do not use the data for unauthorized purposes.
